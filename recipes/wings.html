Game Day Lemon Pepper Wings

Description

Name a more iconic game day food other then wings! I bet you can't! These lemon pepper wings will hit the spot as you cheer your team to victory!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon finely grated lemon zest
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 3 lb. chicken wings, flats, and drumettes separated
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°. Line a rimmed baking sheet with foil or parchment. Place a wire rack in sheet.
  2. In a small bowl, combine lemon zest, baking powder, pepper, garlic powder, and salt.
  3. Pat wings dry and transfer to a large bowl. Add oil and toss to coat. Sprinkle lemon zest mixture over and toss again to coat. Arrange wings on prepared rack, spacing so they do not touch.
  4. Bake wings until crispy and browned, 40 to 50 minutes.
  5. While the wings cook, in a small pot over medium heat, cook butter and honey, stirring, until honey is dissolved into butter, about 3 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in lemon juice.
  6. Let wings cool slightly. Transfer to a clean large bowl. Drizzle lemon butter over, then toss well to coat.
  7. Arrange wings on a platter. Serve with lemon wedges and choice of dipping sauce alongside.
