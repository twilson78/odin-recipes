Game Day Bacon Wrapped Jalepeño Poppers

Description

A game day classic! An app that is sure to please the crowd, easy to make, and a pleasure to eat while watching the game!

Ingredients

  • 1- 8 ounce block of softened cream cheese
  • 1-1/2 cups shredded pepper jack cheese
  • 1 clove garlic
  • Kosher salt
  • Fresh cracked black pepper
  • 12 jalepeños
  • 12 slices of bacon, halved
  • toothpicks, soaked in water
  • Your favorite bbq rub

Directions

  1. Preheat grill to around 350 degrees
  2. Prep the peppers by slicing in half lengthwise, use a spoon to remove the seeds and white membrane (or if you like more heat you can leave as much of the seeds and membrane you'd like).
  3. In a medium bowl, combine the softened cream cheese, shredded cheese, and any other desired seasonings you'd like.
  4. Fill each jalepeño half with the cheese mixture.
  5. Wrap each filled jalepeño half with a piece of bacon and secure it with a soaked toothpick.
  6. Place poppers on the grill in indirect heat and cook for about 20-25 minutes. Toward the last few minutes you can move them to direct heat to get a nice char.
  7. Remove from heat, let cool, and enjoy!
